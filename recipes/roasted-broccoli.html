Roasted Broccoli

Description

This recipe, originally titled "Easy Roasted Broccoli", was submitted to All Recipes by user karenatlincoln. This easy and delicious recipe takes only 30 minutes in total.

This version of the recipse includes an optional step.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Optional: Lemon or garlic yoghurt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C.
  2. Cut broccoli into similar sizes, including both florets and stems.
  3. Mix all broccoli pieces with olive oil.
  4. Put into a baking dish or baking sheet, then add salt and peper.
  5. Roast until broccoli is tender and lightly browned, which sohlud take about 18-20 minutes.
  6. Optional: Depending on your taste, you can squeeze half a lemon on the broccoli or serve with garlic yoghurt.
