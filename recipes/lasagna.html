World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe was submitted to All Recipes by John Chandler. It remains among their most popular recipes.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion, one
  • Garlic, two cloves
  • Tomato - Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg, one

Steps

  1. Cook beef, onion and garlic in a pot until well cooked.
  2. Add tomatoes, salt and seasoning. Simmer for half an hour
  3. Boil a large pot of water. Add salt and boil lasanga noodles for 8-10 minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water.
  4. Mix cheeses, egg and half a teaspoon salt for the sauce.
  5. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat. Then spread a layer of sauce and cheese. On top, put a layer of noodles. Repeat layers and put cheese on top.
  7. Cover the dish with foil. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes.
  8. Serve and enjoy the meal!
